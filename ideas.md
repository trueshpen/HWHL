# Ideas & Tips

## Daily Gestures
- [ ] Leave a sweet note
- [ ] Make her favorite coffee/tea
- [ ] Help with chores without being asked
- [ ] Give a genuine compliment

## Quality Time
- [ ] Plan a date night
- [ ] Watch her favorite show together
- [ ] Cook a meal together
- [ ] Go for a walk and talk

## Thoughtful Acts
- [ ] Remember important dates
- [ ] Surprise her with something she mentioned wanting
- [ ] Listen actively when she talks
- [ ] Support her goals and dreams

## Special Occasions
- [ ] Plan surprises for birthdays/anniversaries
- [ ] Celebrate small victories together
- [ ] Create new traditions
